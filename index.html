Institutional grievance principles

The standards this portal is built to uphold, for every complaint regardless of category.

Confidentiality

Your identity is visible only to the assigned reviewer, never published or shared outside the review process.

Timeliness

Every complaint receives an acknowledgment within 48 hours and a status update at each stage of review.

Non-retaliation

Filing a complaint in good faith carries no academic or administrative penalty, for you or anyone you name.
