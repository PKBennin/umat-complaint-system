UMaT Campus Complaint Portal

Ensuring student concerns are processed transparently, routed automatically to deans and department heads, and resolved with official administrative accountability.

Institutional Grievance Principles

Dean-Led Case Review

Academic, Harassment, and general concerns go directly to the Dean of your Faculty. Financial disputes route to your Faculty Finance Officer. IT issues route to the Central school IT Directorate.

Automated Program Routing

Skip administrative gatekeepers. Simply search and select your degree programme. The system automatically maps your department and routes your ticket to the correct Faculty Dean.

Detailed Appointments & Track

Faculty heads can schedule structured Counselor or In-Person appointments with you. Date, venue, and checklist instructions sync directly into your portal's interactive tracking card.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it mandatory to log in before filing a complaint?

No. We support instant public filings. Any student can file a grievance immediately without an account. Authentication is only required when retrieving details or tracking status.

What credentials should I use to log in to the tracking system?

Use your university Student Index Number and your portal registration password. The system verifies these inputs directly against the prototype school database.

What happens if my complaint is delayed or unresolved?

If your complaint remains unresolved past its urgency limit (e.g., 24 hours for Critical, 48 hours for High), an alert box will appear on your tracker allowing you to click "Resend" to bump it back to the top of the Dean's queue.
